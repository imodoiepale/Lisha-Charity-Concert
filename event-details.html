The Lisha Charity Concert

21 Jan 2023

10 AM - 06 PM

Ramah Centre, Kitale

Lisha Charity Concert 1st Edition

Welcome to the Lisha Charity Concert! We are so excited to bring you an evening of music, entertainment, and giving back to those in need. The Lisha Charity concerts are dedicated to raising funds and awareness for vital resources such as food, water, and medicine. These essential items are often taken for granted by many, but for others, they are a matter of survival.

Our mission is to support organizations that work tirelessly to provide these resources to those in need, both locally and globally. By coming together for this special evening of music and community, we hope to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those who are struggling.

Other Information of Event

We are thrilled to have a lineup of talented musicians and performers who are just as passionate about giving back as we are. We hope that you will join us for this special event and be a part of something truly special. Together, we can make a real impact and bring hope and relief to those who need it most. Thank you for your support and we look forward to seeing you at the Lisha Charity concert!

The proceeds from the Lisha Charity concerts are typically donated to charitable organizations that work to provide food, water, and medicine to those in need, both locally and globally. These organizations may work to provide these resources to communities in need due to poverty, natural disasters, or other challenges. By supporting the Lisha Charity concerts, attendees can contribute to a worthy cause and make a difference in the lives of others.

Edition Partners.

The Lisha Charity concerts would not be possible without the support and partnership of a number of organizations and individuals.These organizations work tirelessly to provide essential resources such as food, water, and medicine to those in need, and we are grateful for their dedication and commitment to making a positive impact on the world.

we are grateful to all of our partners for their support and contributions to the Lisha Charity concerts. Their efforts help to make these events a success and enable us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need.

Why Join This Event?

The Lisha Charity concerts are an opportunity to make a positive impact on the world. By attending these concerts, you can help to raise funds and awareness for essential resources such as food, water, and medicine, and support organizations that provide these resources to those in need. Your attendance and support can make a real difference in the lives of those who are struggling.

The Lisha Charity concerts are also an opportunity to have fun and be entertained. The concerts typically feature a lineup of talented musicians and performers, and offer a fun and enjoyable evening of entertainment.

In addition to these reasons, the Lisha Charity concerts are also a chance to connect with others and be a part of something special. By coming together for a common cause and supporting one another, we can create a sense of community and make a positive impact on the world.

Overall, the Lisha Charity concerts offer a unique and meaningful way to give back, have fun, and be a part of something special. If you are looking for an opportunity to make a difference and be a part of something positive, then joining a Lisha Charity concert is a great choice.
